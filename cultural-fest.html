TechUtsav - The Annual Cultural Fest

Where Talent Meets Tradition and Innovation

Welcome to the Celebration!

TechUtsav is the most awaited event at UCET Hazaribag, a vibrant confluence of art, music, dance, and literature. It's a platform for students to showcase their talents, express their creativity, and celebrate the rich cultural diversity of our institution.

Event Highlights

A glimpse into the extravaganza.

Music & Dance

From solo singing competitions to electrifying group dance performances, feel the rhythm and energy.

Dramatics

Witness captivating stage plays, hilarious skits, and powerful street plays (Nukkad Natak).

Fine Arts

Unleash your creativity in painting, sketching, rangoli making, and other artistic competitions.

Literary Events

Engage in stimulating debates, eloquent poetry slams, and creative writing contests.

Photography

Capture the soul of the fest through your lens in our themed photography competitions.

Fun & Games

Enjoy a variety of informal events, fun-filled games, and challenges throughout the fest.
