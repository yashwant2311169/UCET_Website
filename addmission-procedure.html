B.Tech Admission Procedure

Your Engineering Journey Starts Here

Admissions to the Four-Year B.Tech Degree Programs at University College of Engineering & Technology (UCET), Hazaribag are conducted through the centralized counseling process managed by the Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB). The allocation of seats is based on the All India Rank (AIR) secured by candidates in the JEE (Main) examination conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA).

Eligibility Criteria

  • Academic Qualification: Must have passed the I.Sc / 10+2 or equivalent examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects, along with one of Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology / Technical Vocational subject.
  • Minimum Marks: Must have obtained at least 45% marks (40% marks in case of candidates belonging to reserved categories) in the above subjects taken together.
  • Entrance Exam: Must have appeared in the JEE (Main) examination of the current academic year and possess a valid score/rank.
  • Residency: Must be a citizen of India. Permanent/Local resident of Jharkhand will be given preference as per state government reservation policy.

Step-by-Step Admission Process

The first and foremost step is to register for and appear in the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main conducted by NTA. Your All India Rank in this exam is the basis for admission.

After the declaration of JEE (Main) results, JCECEB releases a notification for online counseling. Candidates must register on the official JCECEB portal within the stipulated time.

During the online counseling process, candidates will need to fill their choices of colleges and engineering branches in order of preference. You should list UCET Hazaribag and your desired branch as one of your top choices.

JCECEB will conduct multiple rounds of seat allotment. The allotment is based on the candidate's JEE (Main) rank, category, and the choices filled. You can check the allotment result by logging into the JCECEB portal.

Candidates who are allotted a seat must download the provisional allotment letter and report to UCET Hazaribag (or the designated reporting center) on the specified dates for physical document verification. After successful verification, you need to pay the admission fee to confirm your seat.
Required Documents Checklist

Candidates must bring the original and two sets of self-attested photocopies of the following documents for verification:

  • JCECEB Seat Allotment Letter
  • JEE (Main) Admit Card
  • JEE (Main) Score Card / Rank Card
  • Class 10th Admit Card, Mark Sheet & Certificate
  • Class 12th Admit Card, Mark Sheet & Certificate
  • School / College Leaving Certificate (SLC/CLC)
  • Migration Certificate
  • Local/Permanent Resident Certificate of Jharkhand
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income and Asset Certificate (for EWS)
  • Aadhar Card
  • Passport-sized Photographs (at least 6)
