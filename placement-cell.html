About the Training & Placement Cell

The Training and Placement Cell at UCET Hazaribag is a dedicated body that plays a pivotal role in shaping the careers of our students. We are committed to bridging the gap between academia and industry by providing comprehensive training and excellent placement opportunities.

Our goal is to ensure that every student is well-equipped with the necessary technical skills, soft skills, and industry exposure to excel in their professional lives. We work tirelessly to foster strong relationships with a wide range of companies and organize various programs to enhance student employability and secure rewarding job offers.

Training & Development

Our Objectives
  • To enhance the technical skills of students through specialized training programs and workshops.
  • To improve soft skills, including communication, teamwork, and leadership qualities.
  • To facilitate industrial training and internships for students to gain practical experience.
  • To organize guest lectures and seminars by industry experts.
  • To conduct mock interviews and group discussions to prepare students for the recruitment process.
Key Training Activities
  • Industrial Visits to leading companies.
  • Workshops on emerging technologies like AI, IoT, and Data Science.
  • Soft Skill Development Programs.
  • Resume Building and Interview Preparation sessions.
  • Summer Internship Programs.
