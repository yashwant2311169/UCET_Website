B.Tech Fee Structure

As per University Notification

Revised Fee Structure (2024-25 Onwards)

The following fee structure is applicable for students admitted in B.Tech 1st semester (Session 2024-25 onwards) and Lateral Entry 3rd semester (Session 2024-25 onwards).

A. Fee Per Semester

Tuition Fee ₹ 25,000
Development Fee ₹ 5,000
Total Semester Fee ₹ 30,000

B. Fee at the Time of Admission (One Time)

Admission Fee ₹ 5,000
Caution Money (Refundable) ₹ 5,000
Placement Service ₹ 1,000
General Maintenance ₹ 1,000
I-Card, Medical Fee, Group Insurance ₹ 2,000
Total Admission Fee ₹ 14,000
Total Fee for First Semester Students

Therefore, a student admitted to the first semester will pay a total of ₹ 44,000 (₹ 30,000 Semester Fee + ₹ 14,000 One-Time Admission Fee).

Payment Information

  • All fees should be paid online through the official University Payment Portal.
  • Payment can be made using Net Banking, Debit/Credit Cards, or UPI.
  • Fees must be paid within the first 15 days of the commencement of the semester.
  • A late fee will be applicable as per university rules for payments made after the due date.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

While the university fee structure is uniform, students from reserved categories can apply for various government scholarships to cover their expenses.

  • Students from Jharkhand belonging to SC, ST, and OBC categories can apply for post-matric scholarships through the e-Kalyan Portal.
  • Other national scholarships are also available on the National Scholarship Portal (NSP).
