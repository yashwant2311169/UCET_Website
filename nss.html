National Service Scheme (NSS)

"Not Me, But You"

About NSS at Vinoba Bhave University

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a Central Sector Scheme of the Government of India, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports. It provides an opportunity for students of Vinoba Bhave University to take part in various government-led community service activities & programmes. The sole aim of the NSS is to provide hands-on experience to young students in delivering community service.

The NSS unit at UCET, Hazaribag, under the guidance of VBU, actively organizes and participates in a variety of social outreach programs, awareness campaigns, and volunteer activities. Our students engage in projects related to environmental conservation, health and sanitation, literacy drives, and emergency relief work, fostering a sense of social responsibility and leadership.

Aims & Objectives

The main objective of the National Service Scheme is to develop the personality of students through community service. It aims to instill the idea of social welfare in students and to provide service to society without bias.

  • Understand the community in which they work.
  • Understand themselves in relation to their community.
  • Identify the needs and problems of the community and involve them in the problem-solving process.
  • Develop among themselves a sense of social and civic responsibility.
  • Utilize their knowledge in finding practical solutions to individual and community problems.
  • Develop competence required for group-living and sharing of responsibilities.
  • Gain skills in mobilizing community participation.
  • Acquire leadership qualities and a democratic attitude.
  • Develop the capacity to meet emergencies and natural disasters.
  • Practice national integration and social harmony.
