Our History & Foundation

Established in 2009, University College of Engineering and Technology (UCET) Hazaribagh was founded with the vision to provide top-tier technical education in Jharkhand. Under the direct management of Vinoba Bhave University, we have grown into a premier institution approved by AICTE and UGC, committed to shaping the future of engineering.

State-of-the-Art Campus

Our sprawling campus is equipped with modern infrastructure, including advanced laboratories, a vast central library, smart classrooms, and high-speed internet connectivity. We provide an environment that fosters innovation and supports holistic development.

Our Vision for the Future

We aim to be a center of excellence that nurtures technically proficient, ethically sound, and socially responsible engineers. Our goal is to empower graduates to tackle global challenges, drive innovation, and become leaders in the technology industry.
