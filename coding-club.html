The UCET Coding Community

Innovate. Collaborate. Code.

From Ideas to Reality

The Coding Clubs at UCET are a student-run ecosystem for aspiring developers and programmers. We foster a culture of learning, collaboration, and innovation, helping students bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world application.

Our Domains

Find your passion and grow your skills.

Competitive Programming

Sharpen your problem-solving skills with data structures and algorithms. Prepare for contests like ICPC and CodeJam.

Web & App Development

Build amazing websites and mobile applications. Learn the latest frameworks and technologies in frontend and backend.

AI & Machine Learning

Dive into the world of artificial intelligence, data science, and neural networks. Work on projects that learn and predict.

What We Do

Learning and growing together through various events.

  • Hands-on Workshops
  • Hackathons & Coding Contests
  • Project Showcases & Idea Pitching
  • Peer-to-Peer Learning Sessions
  • Guest Lectures from Industry Experts
